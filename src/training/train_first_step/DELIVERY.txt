================================================================================
                        LOSS FUNCTIONS
================================================================================

✓ L1 RECONSTRUCTION LOSS
   Minimizes: |M_predicted - M_target|_1
   Purpose: Accurate mel spectrogram prediction
   Weight: 1.0 (default)

✓ STYLE DIVERSITY LOSS
   Minimizes: ReLU(cos_similarity - (1 - margin))
   Purpose: Prevent style embedding collapse
   Weight: 0.5 (default)

✓ TOTAL LOSS
   L_total = 1.0 * L1_recon + 0.5 * L_diversity
   Result: Balanced training with accurate mel and diverse style
